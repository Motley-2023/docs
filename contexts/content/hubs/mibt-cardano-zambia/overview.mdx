## Overview

The MIBT Cardano Hub Zambia is a community-driven innovation hub under the MIBT Global
Ecosystem, empowering youth, women, educators, professionals, and MSMEs to innovate
within the Cardano and Web3 ecosystem.

## Focus
- Web3 & Blockchain Education
- Hackathons & Innovation
- Sustainability & Research
- AfCFTA-aligned Digital Solutions

## Location
Kitwe, Zambia
